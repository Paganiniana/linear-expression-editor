A line can be expressed with the formula y = mx + b.

In this case we would say that m is the slope of the line and b is where it crosses the Y-Axis. So, if we have a very big number for m, then the slope of our line will be VERY steep! On the other hand, if our slope is very small (as close to 0 as you can get!) it will be very shallow.

The same thing goes for b! If it were a very large number, then our whole line would move up and up and up!
